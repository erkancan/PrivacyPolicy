Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 7, 2025

Pumping Tracker (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) What this policy covers

This policy applies to the Pumping Tracker iOS app, websites or pages that link to it, and customer support interactions.

2) Information we collect

a) Information you add

  • Feeding & pumping logs: times, duration, side (left/right/both), volumes, notes, bottle source (fresh/fridge/freezer), reminders, goals.
  • Profiles: baby name or nickname, birth date or due date (optional), and any labels you choose to add.
  • Support messages: content of emails you send to us.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Device data: device model, OS version, app version, language, region, time zone.
  • Usage & diagnostics: basic, de-identified analytics such as feature usage and crash logs to improve stability and UX.

Sensitive data note: Feeding and pumping logs may be considered health-related information. We only process this data to provide the app’s core features (tracking, insights, reminders) and never sell it.

3) How we use information

  • Provide core features (logging, charts, insights, reminders, freezer expiry notifications).
  • Personalize in-app suggestions (e.g., best time windows based on your history).
  • Maintain safety, debug issues, and prevent abuse.
  • Communicate with you about updates or support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4) Legal bases (EEA/UK/CH)

  • Contract: to provide the app and features you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to improve the app (e.g., aggregate, de-identified analytics) and ensure security.
  • Consent: for optional notifications or where required by law. You can revoke consent in iOS Settings or in-app.

5) Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, cloud infrastructure) under confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal or safety recipients if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, we will notify you and your rights will continue to be protected.

6) Data retention

  • Feeding/pumping logs and profiles are retained until you delete them in the app or request deletion.
  • Diagnostics and crash logs are typically retained for up to 24 months, unless a longer period is required for security, compliance, or debugging.

7) Your choices & rights

  • Access, rectify, delete: you can edit or delete your data in the app. You can also request a copy or deletion by emailing erkancanmac@gmail.com.
  • Opt-out of analytics/notifications: control in the app and iOS Settings > Notifications / Privacy.
  • EEA/UK/CH users: you may have rights to object or restrict processing, and to data portability. You can lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
  • California residents: you may have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including access and deletion. We do not sell or “share” your data for cross-context behavioral advertising.

8) Children’s privacy

The app is designed for use by adults (parents/caregivers). We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, contact us to delete it.

9) International transfers

We may process data on servers located outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect your information.

10) Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect your data (e.g., encryption in transit, access controls). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please use strong device passcodes and keep backups.

11) Third-party links

The app may link to third-party resources (e.g., help articles). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or laws. We will post the new effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
