Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

We (“we”, “us”, or “our”) are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect via our mobile applications, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) What this policy covers

This policy applies to the mobile applications developed by us (collectively, the "Services" or "App"), websites or pages that link to it, and customer support interactions.

2) Information we collect

a) Information you add

  • User Content & Logs: Data you explicitly enter into the App depending on its functionality (e.g., tasks, notes, health logs, preferences, timers, goals).
  • Profiles: Names, dates, or other profile information you create within the App.
  • Support messages: Content of emails you send to us for support or feedback.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Device data: Device model, OS version, app version, language, region, time zone.
  • Usage & diagnostics: Basic, de-identified analytics such as feature usage and crash logs to improve stability and UX.

Note on Sensitive Data: Some of our Apps may allow you to track health or biometric data. We only process this data to provide the App’s core features (tracking, insights, reminders) locally on your device or via secure cloud storage if enabled. We never sell your personal data.

3) How we use information

  • Provide core features (logging, charts, insights, reminders, notifications).
  • Personalize in-app suggestions based on your usage history.
  • Maintain safety, debug issues, and prevent abuse.
  • Communicate with you about updates or support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4) Legal bases (EEA/UK/CH)

  • Contract: To provide the App and features you request.
  • Legitimate interests: To improve the App (e.g., aggregate, de-identified analytics) and ensure security.
  • Consent: For optional notifications, tracking, or where required by law. You can revoke consent in iOS Settings or in-app.

5) Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, cloud infrastructure) under confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal or safety recipients if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, we will notify you and your rights will continue to be protected.

6) Data retention

  • User content and profiles are retained until you delete them in the App or request deletion.
  • Diagnostics and crash logs are typically retained for up to 24 months, unless a longer period is required for security, compliance, or debugging.

  • Access, rectify, delete: You can edit or delete your data in the App. You can also request a copy or deletion by emailing erkancanmac@gmail.com.

  • EEA/UK/CH users: You may have rights to object or restrict processing, and to data portability. You can lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
  • California residents: You may have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including access and deletion. We do not sell or “share” your data for cross-context behavioral advertising.
